Intro

What defines the word hobby? When people think of hobby,
they usually think of any physical activity that could be labeled as fun.
While these people are true, personally I feel a hobby doesn't have
to be a physical activity; it can be anything that brings you pleasure and joy!
Based on the definition above, I hereby bring you three of my hobbies.
